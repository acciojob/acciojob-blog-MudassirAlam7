Brute Force vs. Optimization

Brute force optimization serves as a fundamental approach in algorithm design, particularly for small-scale problems or when accuracy is critical. However, due to its inefficiency for larger datasets, it is often supplemented or replaced with more advanced techniques. Understanding brute force methods can provide valuable insights into the development of efficient algorithms.
